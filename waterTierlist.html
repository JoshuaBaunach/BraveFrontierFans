Tier List - Water

This is the tier list for water units. Information about the tiers are shown below:

  • Meta: These units have fantastic skills AND unprecedented stats. Almost irreplaceable on certain squad setups. Raise ASAP.
  • Elite: Elite: Although these units didn’t have enough “oomph” to make it up to meta, these units are still excellent since they sport great stats and great abilities. Raise with confidence that you’re getting a sweet unit.
  • High: Units in this category have good stats and/or good abilities. Although those stats/abilities may not be as fantastic as those in the upper tiers, they are still nice to have. Raise with confidence.

Note that this list only accounts for the units in the top three tiers that are currently in the Global servers. If you do not see your unit here, then (a) they didn't make it to the top three or (b) they aren't in the global servers yet.

Be sure to look your unit up if they fall under one of those two categories; they may actually be a great unit!
